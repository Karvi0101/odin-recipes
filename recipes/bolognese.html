Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

A simple bolognese recipe full of flavor. Takes about an hour to make, the longer you let it simmer the better.

Ingredients:

  • Minced meat 500gr
  • Tomato Sauce 400gr
  • Carrots 2
  • Onions 2
  • Garlic 2 cloves
  • Salt
  • Bay leaves 2-3
  • Pepper
  • Oregano

Steps:

  1. Cut the onion and put it to simmer in a deep pan with some olive oil
  2. While the onion is cooking, shred the carrots and add them to the pan
  3. Finely chop the garlic and add that.
  4. Once the vegetables are soft (not cooked) add the minced meat and break it up
  5. Let it cook for 5-10 minutes, then add a spoonfull of tomato paste.
  6. Add the jar of chopped tomatoes and let it simmer for 3-4 minutes. Then fill the jar with water and add that too
  7. Add salt pepper and oregano for flavor and let it simmer for 30-45 minutes (until desired thickness of the sauce is reached)
